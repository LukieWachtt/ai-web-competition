Overview on how

Humans managed to use AIs to fight against their biggest threat.

0.1

Monitor Deforestations

Deforestation

has been a growing issue for humanity due to its devastating environmental, economic and social impacts. AI plays a crucial role in preventing deforestation by monitoring forests and detecting illegal activities in real-time. Using satellite imagery, drones, and machine learning algorithms, AI can identify changes in forest cover and alert authorities to illegal logging or land clearing. Additionally, AI-powered predictive models help forecast areas at risk of deforestation, allowing for proactive measures to protect vulnerable ecosystems. These technologies not only improve forest conservation efforts but also support global initiatives to combat climate change and preserve biodiversity.

0.2

Tracking Wildlife Populations

Tracking Wildlife.

AI is transforming how we track wildlife populations, helping to protect endangered species and maintain biodiversity. By analyzing data from camera traps, drones, and satellite images, AI can identify and monitor animals in their natural habitats, providing real-time updates on population numbers and movements. This technology helps conservationists detect population declines, track migration patterns, and identify poaching activities. With AI, wildlife tracking becomes more accurate and efficient, enabling better conservation efforts and informed decisions to protect threatened species and ecosystems.

0.3

Predicting Natural Disasters

Natural disasters

have long been a significant issue for humanity due to their devastating effects on life, property, and economies. AI is revolutionizing the prediction of natural disasters, making it possible to forecast events like earthquakes, floods, and hurricanes with greater accuracy. By analyzing vast amounts of data from weather patterns, seismic activity, and satellite images, AI models can detect early warning signs and predict where and when disasters are likely to occur. This technology allows authorities to issue timely warnings, enabling communities to prepare and reduce the impact of these events. Meet the AIs who changed the world.

0.1

Global Forest Watch

GFW uses satellite imagery and machine learning algorithms to monitor forests in real-time. It provides data on deforestation, land use changes, and illegal logging activities. The platform alerts governments and NGOs to illegal activities, helping to improve forest conservation efforts.

0.2

IBM's Weather Company

IBM's Weather Company uses AI and machine learning to analyze weather patterns and historical data to improve disaster prediction and response. Their models help forecast natural disasters like hurricanes and floods, providing real-time alerts to authorities and communities to enhance preparedness and reduce damage.

0.3

Wildlife Insights

Wildlife Insights uses AI to analyze images from camera traps and drones to monitor wildlife populations. The platform identifies animal species and tracks their movements, providing critical data for conservationists to make informed decisions about protecting endangered species.
